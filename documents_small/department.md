# Departments
:::
:::

![banner](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/design/front/assets/images/about-banner.jpg){.img-fluid
width="1920px" height="343px"}
:::

::: {role="main"}
::: {.section .faculty-section}
::: container
::: desc
We help students achieve academic excellence in a diverse range of
disciplines and fields -- through our four unique Faculties. Each
Faculty is made up of distinct schools and areas of research. The
faculty structure enables high-quality teaching by gathering together
academic expertise, and also encourages interdisciplinary research
across faculties.
:::

::: faculty-row
::: item
::: card
![](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/2023/02/27/4617c2014f158a0fdcb7517411eb0d9a.webp){.img-fluid
width="706px" height="477px"}

::: card-body
#### [Computer Science & Engineering](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/department/cse)

The programme is designed to create innovators and researchers. Students
are given a strong foundation in CSE that includes electronics and
hardware. The B.Tech. (CSE) program at IIIT-Guwahati starts with
computing oriented courses first, and allows the possibility of doing
science courses later.
:::
:::
:::

::: item
::: card
![](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/2023/02/27/606a5d4c9353b937af6b5ae17bab18c4.webp){.img-fluid
width="706px" height="477px"}

::: card-body
#### [Electronics & Communication Engineering](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/department/ece)

The programme is designed to create innovators and researchers. ECE
courses like Electronics, Signals & Systems, Communications, VLSI, and
embedded systems are introduced in the early semesters. The main
objectives of the B.Tech.(ECE) program are to produce students who are
well prepared for Indian industry with necessary core competency to
succeed long-term in engineering/entrepreneurship careers (post
B.Tech.), and who are well prepared to undertake PG studies and research
careers.
:::
:::
:::

::: item
::: card
![](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/2023/02/27/830900a0d2dff28a2367c3bcb5ea48c6.webp){.img-fluid
width="706px" height="477px"}

::: card-body
#### [Science & Mathematics](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/department/science-mathematics)

The Department of Science and Mathematics at IIIT Guwahati started in
2013 with the aim to groom mathematically sound young minds for problem
solving in industry and other concerns. The department has been
vigorously engaged in research and teaching in relevant areas of
Mathematics, Statistics and Computer Science.
:::
:::
:::

::: item
::: card
![](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/2023/02/27/268a2c19eccab7f84db2ac4a5e0e5f65.webp){.img-fluid
width="706px" height="477px"}

::: card-body
#### [Humanities & Social Sciences](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/department/humanities-social-sciences)

The Department of Humanities and Social Sciences of IIITG was formally
established on 31 March 2016. It currently has five faculty members who
teach various compulsory and elective courses at the undergraduate level
in different disciplines of HSS such as English, Linguistics, Economics
and Political Science.
:::
