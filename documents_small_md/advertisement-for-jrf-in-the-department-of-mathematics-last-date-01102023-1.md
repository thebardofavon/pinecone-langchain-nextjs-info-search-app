#### JRF Position (August 2023)

Applications are invited for JRF position in Mathematics in the
following area of research:

  Sl No   Area                                     Faculty                                                                                           Category    Assistantship          Number
  ------- ---------------------------------------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ----------- ---------------------- --------
  1       Coding Theory (Topological, Algebraic)   [Dipendu Maity](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/science-and-mathemetics/dipendu-maity){target="_blank"}   Full-time   Fellowship from NBHM   1

##### ELIGIBILITY

Applicant must fulfil both of the following criteria:

1.  Master's degree in Mathematics in the relevant area with a minimum
    Cumulative Performance Index (CPI) of 6.5 or 60% of marks
2.  Selected through National Eligibility Test - CSIR-UGC NET including
    Lecturership (Assistant Professorship) and GATE.

##### RESERVATIONS

(SC, ST, OBC (Non-creamy layer) and PD categories): Eligibility criteria
will be relaxed by 5% marks or 0.5 CPI for SC/ST/PD applicants.
Reservation is as per GOI rules. OBC (Non-creamy layer) candidates will
have to enclose certificate and self-declaration statement as per format
at Annexure-I & Annexure-II. The OBC (NCL) Certificate, issued by the
competent authority, on or after 01-06-2019, shall only be acceptable.

##### FELLOWSHIP

As per the fund assigned to this project from NBHM.

##### THE SCHEDULE AND THE PROGRAMME

1.  Admissions will be held based on applications received. The last
    date of receiving the application will be 01-11-2023 (Wednesday).
2.  Date of Selection in Online Mode: 10-11-2023 (Friday)
3.  Shortlisting will be done based on the availability of seats and the
    academic performance of the candidates. A candidate merely
    fulfilling the eligibility criteria may not be shortlisted.
4.  There will be no support for travel for the interview.
5.  There is no application fee.
6.  The JRF position will terminate at the end of project.

\*The JRF needs to pay the hostel and mess fees only when a JRF joins a
hostel. Hostel rent will be charged on a pro-rata basis (semester of 6
months with a charge of 12,500/- for the whole semester) based on when
the JRF joins a hostel.

##### SUBMISSION

Interested candidates are requested to apply by filling up the
Application form along with the self-attested copies of all degrees;
mark sheets and GATE / NET score card copy and mail it to
dipendu@iiitg.ac.in

Application form [To download form click
here](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/files/PhD_JRF_application_form2023_August.doc)
