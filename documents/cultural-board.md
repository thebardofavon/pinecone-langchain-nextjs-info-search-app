# ABOUT IIITG'S CULTURAL BOARD {#itua .heading}

IIIT Guwahati boasts a vibrant cultural environment, where we strongly
believe that cultural activities not only shape individuals\'
personalities but also provide stress relief and help uncover hidden
talents. Under the guidance of the Cultural Secretary, our dedicated
club coordinators ensure active participation of the entire college in
cultural activities organised by the nine diverse cultural clubs on
campus.

The clubs include Tarang-Music Club, D-Dazzelers -Dance Club,
Afsaane-Drama Club, Kalrav\--Literary Club, Overbooked-Reading Club,
Alankrit-Fine Art Club, Portraiture-Photography Club, Prashnatantra-Quiz
Club, and Bios Skopien-Movie Club. Each club is managed by two
coordinators who facilitate smooth operations and organise weekly
events. The Cultural Board, consisting of a Faculty Coordinator and two
Associate Faculty Coordinators, the Cultural Secretary, SGC and Club
Coordinators, collaborates to enhance and create the best cultural
environment at IIIT Guwahati. Our aim is to provide students with a
platform to explore their interests, showcase their talents, and foster
a thriving cultural community within the institute.
